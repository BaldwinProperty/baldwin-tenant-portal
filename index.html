Trash & Recycling

7 AM on Friday

Please place receptacles at curb Thursday night & return to house within 24 hours of pickup.

If a holiday falls on or prior to your collection day for the week, service will be delayed by 1 day.
If a holiday falls on a Saturday or Sunday, there will be NO delay in service.

Observed Holidays: Memorial Day, 4th of July, Labor Day, Thanksgiving, Christmas Day, New Year's Day

What items CAN be recycled?

*Paper, Plastics #1-7, Metals, Glass.

-Please empty & rinse all containers.
-Place recyclables loose and unbagges in your curbside recycling container.
-Flatten all cardboard and paperboard boxes.
-Place your recylcing container 4 feet from parked cars and other obstacles.

What items CAN NOT be recycled?

*Plastic bags, plastic film, styrofoam, garbage, electronics, scrap metal, hazardous waste.
